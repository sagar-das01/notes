Thinking like a hacker

Hacking is not bad rather encouraged. Hacking helps to better securing our technologies.
With the help of hacking, we can use a sample computing system to assess,

  • High Level Risks
  • Vulnerabilities
  • Attack Vectors
What is hacking according to the hacker lexicon?

As a hacker we must do the following -

  1. Categorize assets, risks, threats, Vulnerabilities and exploits
  2. Identify different types of vulnerabilities in a system
  3. Identify the categories of a cyber threat
  4. Determine the phase of a cyber attack
  5. Recognize common exploits
